🏆 Contest Rules
  • 🥇
    Each team qualified for playoffs will be awarded with 5 points.
  • 🔒
    Playoff predictions are locked and winner will be decided based on playoffs qualified teams.
  • ⏱️
    Cutoff time will be considered for each daily predictions and voting post cutoff will not be considered.
  • 🕒
    Cutoff time for match prediction is before match starts and for other contests it will be based on the poll and decided by organizer.
  • 📊
    Each daily match win prediction 2 points and loss will be -1, in play off it will 3 points and loss -2.
  • 💡
    Each other poll prediction will be +2 points and no loss prediction points.
  • 🚫
    Match cancelled scenarios: related polls get cancelled.
  • ⚡
    Tie break and super over scenario: new poll will be announced; fastest fingers first will get the chance to contest.
